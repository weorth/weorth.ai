Athena

Athena is a state-of-the-art, high-performance server system designed to facilitate the training and fine-tuning of AI models, enabling optimal performance and accuracy.

Threadripper Pro 5955WX Processor: Athena is driven by the impressive AMD Threadripper Pro 5955WX processor, featuring 16 cores and a clock speed ranging from 4.0 to 4.5 GHz. This processing powerhouse provides the computational muscle needed to handle complex AI training tasks efficiently.
NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090 GPU: The server system is equipped with the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090 GPU, boasting a massive 24 GB of VRAM. This GPU is optimized for AI workloads and enables lightning-fast parallel processing, accelerating the training process and allowing for the development of highly accurate AI models.
256 GB RAM: With a substantial 256 GB of RAM, Athena can handle large-scale AI model training and fine-tuning with ease. This generous memory capacity ensures that data scientists can work on complex AI tasks without being limited by memory constraints.
High-Capacity NVMe Storage: Athena features two high-speed 3.84TB NVMe drives, providing ample storage space for datasets, models, and intermediate results. The ultra-fast NVMe technology ensures quick data access, minimizing bottlenecks during the training process.
Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) and Bluetooth 5.2: Athena is equipped with Wi-Fi 6 technology, ensuring high-speed wireless connectivity for data transfer, updates, and remote access. Bluetooth 5.2 support enables seamless peripheral connectivity and device interaction.
HDMI Monitor, USB Keyboard, and USB Mouse: Athena comes complete with an HDMI monitor, USB keyboard, and USB mouse, allowing users to interact with the system effortlessly. The included peripherals provide a convenient and user-friendly setup for controlling and monitoring AI training sessions.
Athena is a game-changing solution for AI model development, enabling accuracy unlike anything every before!
